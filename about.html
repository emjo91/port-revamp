I am a graduate of Omaha Code School from the April 2015 Class, and I also have an Associate's degree in Visual Publications from Southeast Community College.

More About Me...

While getting my degree in Visual Publications (read as: Graphic Design), I took a few web designing classes and was introduced to code. After learning a few of the basics in HTML and CSS something sparked for me, and I discovered how much I really liked to code. I took it upon myself to learn more about HTML and CSS, googling anything that I could to find out more. In 2014, I found out about Omaha Code School, and decided to apply. By October of 2014, I found out I would be participating in the January 2015 class! For 12 weeks, I immersed myself into code culture, and learned how to code in Ruby, and use Ruby on Rails among other languages.

Aside from being a web developer, some of my hobbies/things I like include: Free Style Disc Dog, DDR (That's Dance Dance Revolution), videogames, motorcycles, drawing/designing, learning to play the guitar, and road trips.

I am the proud owner of two amazing pups! I have an English Shepherd named Kobayashi Maru, and a rescue Heeler mix named Phoebe. They're absolutely crazy, but I couldn't image life without them!
