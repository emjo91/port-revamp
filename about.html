More About Me...

All stories have a beginning; mine starts as a young teen trying to outdo her friends in a battle of winning the honor of having the best Myspace layout. Sure, you could choose from pre-made templates, that would be the easy thing to do. I would accept no less than totally custom. Little by little I started to teach myself HTML and CSS; my passion for design and development only grew from there. Fast forward nearly ten years later and we have someone who still loves playing with code and designing new and amazing things. Myspace started an obsession, which I'm proud to say has become a career.

I do other normal things, too. Some of my other interests include, but are not limited to: Riding motorcycles (mostly just mine), freestyle disc dog, DDR (that's Dance Dance Revolution for those of you who aren't savvy), videogames, adventuring with my pups.

I would also like to point out that I am the proud owner/parent of two very amazing dogs! Phoebe or Foo is a four year old rescued Australian Cattle Dog mix. Kobayashi Maru or Kobi is a three year old English Shepherd. Both of them mean the world to me, and if asked about them I could talk about them for days. This is meant more as a warning than a fun fact.
